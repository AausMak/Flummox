How Dose It Work?

  • STEP 1

    Discovery

    At Flummox, we start by conducting in-depth interviews with you to understand your business goals, target audience, and requirements for your website. This helps us tailor the design and development process to meet your specific needs.

    Next
  • STEP 2

    Research

    We research competitors in your industry to determine what design and functionality features are standard in the market. This helps us create a website that is competitive and aligned with industry standards.

    Next
  • STEP 3

    Planning

    We create a comprehensive plan that outlines the website's structure, features, and functionality. This includes a timeline, budget, and project milestones that we'll work together to achieve.

    Next
  • STEP 4

    Design

    Using wireframes and mockups, we'll design the website to be user-friendly, aesthetically pleasing, and aligned with your branding and messaging. We work collaboratively with you to make necessary revisions and ensure you're satisfied with the design.

    Next
  • STEP 5

    Development

    Once the design is approved, we'll begin the development process. This includes coding the website, integrating features, and testing for bugs and errors. We'll optimize the website for speed, usability, and search engine visibility.

    Next
  • STEP 6

    Launch

    After development, we'll thoroughly test the website and make sure it's ready to go live. We'll launch the website and ensure it's running smoothly.

    Next
  • STEP 7

    Maintenance

    We offer ongoing maintenance and support for the website, including regular updates with fresh content, monitoring performance, and making necessary changes to ensure the website remains functional and up-to-date.

    Next
  • STEP 8

    Feedback

    We'll solicit feedback from you throughout the design and development process to ensure the final product meets your needs and expectations.

    Next
  • STEP 9

    Improvement

    We're always looking for ways to improve our design and development process to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technology.
